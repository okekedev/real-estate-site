Meet Jared

Born on the fairway,
built for this market.

Placeholder Image

Born and raised in Holliday, Texas, Jared is proud to call North Texas home. After earning All-American honors as a golfer at Midwestern State University, he spent five years building a successful career in sales before teaming up with his wife, Kayle, in real estate.

Jared brings a calm, patient, and personable approach to every transaction — whether guiding first-time homebuyers, working with investors, or navigating his own rental properties. Known for his easygoing nature and genuine care for people, he ensures every client feels supported from the first showing to the closing table.

When he's not helping clients find their next home, you can catch him on the golf course — and rumor has it, every home purchase comes with a free golf lesson.
